Serious cryptography. Zero network.

Your vault is a single encrypted file on your Mac. Nobody — not even Filaxy™ Labs — can read it. Because it's open source, anyone can verify exactly that.

  • Argon2idMemory-hard key derivation, slow by design to defeat guessing.
  • XChaCha20-Poly1305Authenticated encryption that also detects any tampering.
  • No network, everNo accounts, no servers, no analytics. The app works fully offline.
  • Open source (MIT)Every line is public and auditable on GitHub.
